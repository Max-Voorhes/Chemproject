What's Your Number Project

This is a project all about the chemistry of an acoustic guitar, specifically the strings of a guitar and the body of a guitar. I had three main questions that I wanted answered. 1. How many copper atoms are in a bronze guitar string, 2. How many tin atoms are in a bronze guitar string, and 3. How many Carbon atoms are inside of the spruce top of an acoustic guitar?
